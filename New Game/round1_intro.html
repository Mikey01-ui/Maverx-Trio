Mission Control

Alright rookie, first things first. Before we can steal anything, we need to know where everything is. MegaCorp's vault contains thousands of datasets scattered across different departments. We need to map this place out.
I've pulled some intel on their data governance structure. Each department owns specific datasets - HR handles employee info, Finance manages the money data, Sales tracks customer relationships. Your job? Match the datasets to their owners.
Look at the metadata, check the data lineage, and use your head. Get this right and we'll know exactly where to find what we need. Mess it up, and we'll be wandering blind in a digital maze when the clock is ticking.

YOUR OBJECTIVE

  • Review 10 different datasets with their descriptions and metadata
  • Identify which department owns each dataset based on the clues
  • Drag and drop each dataset to the correct department
  • Score points for correct matches (100 points per match)
  • Complete within the time limit to maximize your score

WHAT TO EXPECT

10 Datasets
6 Departments
8 Minutes
1000 Points Max
