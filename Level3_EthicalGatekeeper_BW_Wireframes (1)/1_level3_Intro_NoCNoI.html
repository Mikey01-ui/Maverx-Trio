The Challenge

You've been brought in as a Data Privacy Consultant at MedVault Health, a growing healthcare organization. A recent audit has revealed that several datasets containing patient and employee information are being used across departments without proper privacy assessments.

Your task is to evaluate each dataset's sensitivity, inspect its privacy metadata, and determine the correct ethical action. Some data can be used freely, while other datasets require anonymization, explicit consent, or must not be used at all.

Your Mission
Review 8 datasets with varying sensitivity levels. Inspect their privacy metadata, assess legal and ethical constraints, and assign each dataset to the correct ethical action zone. Only compliant decisions will allow you to progress.
Icon

Learning Objectives

  • 1
    Recognize Sensitive Data
    Identify datasets that contain personal, sensitive, or regulated information requiring special handling.
  • 2
    Assess Privacy Constraints
    Evaluate legal basis, consent requirements, and retention policies before making data usage decisions.
  • 3
    Apply Privacy Principles
    Practice consent, data minimization, anonymization, and purpose limitation in realistic scenarios.
  • 4
    Make Ethical Decisions Under Pressure
    Weigh risks and interpret regulations to make responsible, ethics-driven decisions with time constraints.
