The Challenge

After successfully organizing the company's data landscape, you're now facing your next challenge. The leadership team has prepared multiple dashboards filled with complex visualizations, charts, and tables showing business performance.

However, the data contains subtle inconsistencies, missing values, and ambiguous patterns. Your task is to cut through the noise, identify meaningful trends, and select the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that truly matter for strategic decision-making.

Your Mission
Analyze interactive dashboards, interpret complex data visualizations, and identify the most relevant KPIs and trends. Distinguish between signal and noise to support informed business decisions.
OBJ

  • 3
    Select Relevant KPIs
    Choose the most appropriate Key Performance Indicators to support business decision-making.
  • 4
    Apply Analytical Reasoning
    Use critical thinking under time pressure to evaluate quantitative data for strategic insights.
